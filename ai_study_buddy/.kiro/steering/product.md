# AI Study Buddy - Product Overview

AI Study Buddy is a personalized learning platform that helps students improve their academic performance through AI-powered assistance and analytics.

## Core Features

- **Student Profile Management**: Upload academic records and syllabus files to create personalized learning profiles
- **Performance Analytics**: AI-driven analysis of student strengths, weaknesses, and performance patterns
- **AI Chat Tutor**: Interactive conversational AI that provides step-by-step explanations and learning support
- **Study Planning**: Automated generation of personalized study schedules based on performance data
- **Practice Quizzes**: Subject-specific quiz generation with explanations
- **Exam Preparation**: Targeted revision notes and preparation materials for weak subjects

## Target Users

- Students seeking personalized academic support
- Learners who need structured study planning
- Users looking for AI-powered tutoring assistance

## Key Value Propositions

- Personalized learning experience based on individual performance data
- AI-powered explanations that adapt to student understanding levels
- Comprehensive study planning and progress tracking
- Interactive and engaging learning interface with dark/light theme support
